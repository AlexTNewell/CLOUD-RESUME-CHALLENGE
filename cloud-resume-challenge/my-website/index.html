Alexander Newell
AWS Cloud Engineer

Hello, I'm Alex and I'm an AWS cloud and DevOps engineer.

I'm from Cambridge but now I'm based in Westminster, London. I began coding and using containers back in 2012 in my school days, when I wanted to run Darthmod Empire Total War, a Windows-based mod, on a mac.

After graduating from Trinity College in Dublin, I worked for Ernst and Young as a Due Diligence and Valuations Analyst. I then worked for SMBC Bank in their Treasury FX derivatives department.

Quite by surprise, following the lifting of Covid restrictions, an opportunity arose to work in the TV & Film Industry, which I did from October 2021 until December 2022.

After we wrapped on the last film, I really wanted to challenge myself and I'd always loved coding and building Tech. So I decided to pursue a career in IT and since then I have had the privilege to build software for some wonderful clients.

I achieved my AWS Cloud Practitioner certification in February and my AWS Solutions Architect Associate certification in April, and I am now working towards my AWS Developer Associate certification (scheduled for June 2023).

When I’m not at the computer, I'm usually running, swimming or in the gym.
